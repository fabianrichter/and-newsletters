Over the past 17 years, the Innovation Forum for Automation has earned a reputation of providing an excellent opportunity for networking and personal exchange between industry experts. We will continue on this path, however, the current situation requires a new approach: a completely digital event. Driven by increasing demand for automation our next steps are dedicated to creating an even more engaging virtual forum.
The Innovation Forum for Automation as you know it, will be transformed into a series of events in the first four month of 2021. Each of the 4 partner companies of the Automation Network Dresden (AND - Fabmatics, Kontron AIS, SYSTEMA and XENON) will host one event, arranging it individually from the agenda up to the technical organization. The link tying it all together is the mission the AND is committed to: Keeping the community connected and up-to-date with new automation trends across high-tech industries and innovative projects.
And what do you get out of it? A digital AND session every last Thursday of the month from January to April 2021. Each session will be packed with news from the world of automation, exciting insights into the hottest trend topics, and input, input, input.
You don’t want to miss this! Save these dates in your calendar:
SYSTEMA: January 28, 2021, 9am-12pm CET
Fabmatics: February 25, 2021
XENON: March 25, 2021
Kontron AIS: April 29, 2021
SYSTEMA will start with a much discussed topic: Datafication and Automation: The new Normal for Semiconductor Manufacturing
Reserve January 28, 2021, 9am-12pm CET in your calendar! Invitations with detailed information are coming your way soon.
